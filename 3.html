Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my PIN?
Go to Settings > Security > Change PIN. You'll need to verify your identity using OTP sent to your registered phone number.
What are the transfer limits?
Daily transfer limit is ₦1,000,000 for transactions to other banks and ₦5,000,000 for Unity Bank transfers.
How do I block my card?
Go to Cards section, select the card you want to block, and tap on "Block Card". You can also contact support immediately.
How do I apply for a loan?
Go to Loans section, check your eligibility, select a loan product, fill the application form, and submit. You'll receive a decision within 24 hours.
